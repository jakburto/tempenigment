My Top Pocket Genealogical Resources

FamilySearch.org ⇐ This FREE resource is a one stop shop for genealogical research. There are tons of guidance for beginners as well as seasoned searchers! Be sure to check out their full-text search to quickly find gems hidden in records.

HistoryGeo.com ⇐ This PAID resource is a great tool to use if you are researching landowners prior to 1900.

GEDmatch.com ⇐ This FREE resource is a treasure trove of advanced DNA analysis tools! Be sure to read all of the options carefully prior to uploading your raw DNA.

mappingthefreedmensbureau.com ⇐ This FREE resource is perfect if you are ready to dig into records of your newly emancipated ancestors!

DNA Painter ⇐ This FREE resource has many innovative tools for investigating connections between DNA matches. I would start with the Shared cM tool to get a better understanding of how your matches may be related to you.
