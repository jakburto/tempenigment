Video: The Interview

Project Description

I produced this video as a final class project during my first semester of graduate school in the fall of 2023. My intent was to produce a short film contrasting formal and informal learning. My target audience consisted of graduate students, like myself, who were new to the field of Instructional Design. As a nod to my professor, I decided to name drop him during a meditation mantra scene to clam the nerves of my character. All elements of the project including, conception, storyboarding, script writing, casting, acting, filming, directing, editing and post production were completed by me.

In the video I play two characters that personify the polar opposite characteristics of formal and informal learning. Both characters are candidates who are interviewed by their potential employer to decide who gets the job. Their style of dress, dialect, and answers to the interview questions all correspond with the type of learning being portrayed. I intentionally played both of the main characters to challenge myself and showcase my creativity and video production skills on a short timeline.
